About Me

Graduate Student | Electrical Engineer | Power Electronics | Wireless Charging | EV Systems

I am a Master’s student in Electrical Engineering at San Diego State University, passionate about power electronics, EV technology, and renewable energy.

During my Electrical Engineering Internship at Hyaxiom Inc. (A Doosan Company), I worked on high-voltage power systems and thermal management, focusing on:

  • Designing HV pre-charge circuits to prevent inrush currents (AutoCAD Electrical)
  • Engineering HV rectifier cooling systems for thermal optimization
  • Assembling & testing HV stack voltage measurement devices
  • Collaborating on actuator integration, VFDs, and PLC control logic

As the Solar & Electrical Sub-system Head at Team Hyperion, I led a 30-member team in designing a solar-powered EV, achieving higher efficiency and winning awards at national & international competitions.

I also interned at Electronet Equipments Pvt Ltd, developing power supply hardware for industrial applications.

💡 Technical Skills: Power Electronics | PCB Design | MATLAB, Simulink | Ansys HFSS | AutoCAD Electrical | Embedded Systems | EV Powertrain & BMS
